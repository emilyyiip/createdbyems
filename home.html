Hi, I'm Emily.

I design things of all sorts, whether that is events, spaces, art, or technology. I like to create in a way that feels intuitive, but also a bit whimsical.

Currently, I am designing stationery for my shop, Created by Ems, and writing about the process behind it. I am also a university student studying data science and environmental science, with an interest in geospatial technology. On campus, I have organized events for students on behalf of Claude by Anthropic. My favorite art mediums are watercolor and oil pastel, but I have recently been enjoying making stained glass and ceramics.

Outside of work, I like to spend time at my church, cooking, baking, or going on long walks. I also really like dogs.
