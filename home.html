Hi, I'm Emily.

I design things of all sorts, whether that is events, spaces, art, or technology. I like to create in a way that feels simple and intuitive, but also a bit whimsical. The packaged version of my creations go by the name Created by Ems.

Currently, I am designing stationery for my shop and writing about the process behind it. I am also a university student studying data science and environmental science, with an interest in geospatial technology. On campus, I have organized events for students on behalf of Claude by Anthropic. My favorite art mediums are watercolor and oil pastel, but I have recently been enjoying making stained glass and ceramics.

Outside of work, I like to spend time at church, cooking, and going on long walks to the beach.
